About the Artist

Mina Arcy

I work primarily in oil and acrylic, drawn to subjects that hold stillness — interiors bathed in afternoon light, objects left behind, the geometry of quiet rooms.

My practice moves between careful observation and instinctive mark-making. I'm interested in the tension between presence and absence, and what a painting can hold without saying.

Based in Brisbane, I exhibit locally and internationally, and take on a limited number of commissions each year.

Work With Me

Commissions

I accept a small number of commissions each year. Each piece is made with care and full attention.

01

Enquire

Send me a message through the contact form describing your vision, size, and timeline. I respond within five business days.

02

Proposal

I'll send a custom proposal with a sketch, material choices, and pricing. A 30% deposit confirms your commission.

03

Delivery

Progress photos are shared throughout. On completion, your painting is carefully packed and shipped or available for collection.
